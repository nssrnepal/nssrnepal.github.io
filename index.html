Research That Shapes Tomorrow

Empowering Young Researchers for Academic and Scientific Advancement — Towards an Informed, Research-Oriented Society.

Get Involved

The Nepalese Society of Student Researchers (NSSR) is a nonprofit, youth-led membership organization established under the Companies Act, 2063 of Nepal. Dedicated to fostering a vibrant research culture in the country, NSSR seeks to nurture aspiring researchers and create a conducive environment for academic inquiry and innovation.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between student researchers and established academics, providing a collaborative platform that promotes dialogue, mentorship, and scholarly engagement. Through this initiative, NSSR aspires to enhance academic excellence and make meaningful contributions to Nepal’s scientific and intellectual advancement. At the heart of our efforts is a commitment to empowering the next generation of scientists with the tools, knowledge, and support necessary to thrive in both national and global research landscapes.

NSSR further recognizes that the pursuit of scientific understanding should not be confined to academia alone. To this end, we are actively engaged in promoting scientific literacy across all strata of Nepalese society—empowering individuals from diverse backgrounds to think critically, engage with scientific knowledge, and make informed decisions in their everyday lives.

Mission, Values, and Ethics

NSSR is committed to advancing scientific inquiry and literacy through a clear mission, strong core values, and unwavering ethical commitments.

NSSR Seeks To:

To Promote Research Culture

Foster an environment that encourages academic inquiry, critical thinking, and innovation among students and early-career researchers.

To Promote Academic Integration and Mentorship

Facilitate meaningful interaction between students, researchers, and established scholars, promoting mentorship and collaborative research.

To Advance Scientific Literacy

Promote public understanding of science and the scientific method across all sectors of society, ensuring that science serves as a tool for informed decision-making.

To Collaborate Nationally and Internationally

Work in partnership with national and international organizations.

To Promote Documentation and Publication

Encourage the collection, creation, editing, and distribution of research materials, scientific literature, and academic publications.

Core Values

Inclusivity and Accessibility

Advocate for equal access to education and scientific opportunities for all, regardless of socio-economic background.

Excellence and Integrity

Uphold the highest standards in academic work, ethical research, and organizational governance.

Collaboration

Engage in partnerships with national and international institutions within the framework of governmental policies and global development goals.

Ethical Commitments

NSSR conducts all activities in adherence to Nepal’s legal framework and global ethical standards. It maintains transparency, accountability, and a strong sense of civic responsibility in every aspect of its operation.
